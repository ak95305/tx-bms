Program Description

The BMS Healthy Clean Program helps mitigate the spread of viruses and germs throughout the office. The program delivers enhanced disinfection in targeted locations, using electrostatic disinfecting technology along with cleaning and wiping of high touch surfaces. BMS’s disinfection practices are recognized by Green Seal for meeting their Guidelines for Safer Covid-19 Disinfecting.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

TXBMS is the industry leader in sustainability. We hold more green certifications than 99% of industry peers. Our dedicated Sustainability department, with LEED AP O&M certified staff, proudly partners with leading green organizations to ensure that our TXBMS Green Clean℠ program exceeds industry standards.

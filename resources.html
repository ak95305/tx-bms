Working at Trident Building Maintenance

Trident Building Maintenance’s goal is to attract and retain the best talent. We believe if you succeed, we all succeed. Our career tracks encourage promotion and upward mobility, no matter where you start.

Our Culture

With maintaining several sites in Washington State, we have screened and educated our staff to handle emergency assignments and repeat services alike - giving us the capability and capacity to meet most facility's needs. Our employees at Trident Building Maintenance are our greatest assets. They come with robust training programs that ensure superior performance with Trident Building Maintenance comprehensive training program. We ensure above average compensation to reward the best performers who in turn stay longer. Our open door policies create an ease of communication, and our flexibility allows us to adapt as needs change.

Safety

The Trident Building Maintenance team is diligent in following the continually updated rules and recommendations from health authorities and meeting the requirements set out by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA).
